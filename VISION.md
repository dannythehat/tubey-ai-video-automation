## Stock Footage System

### How It Works
1. **User Enables** - "Enhance with stock footage" toggle (default: OFF)
2. **GPT-5 Analyzes** - Identifies transition points in script
3. **Smart Selection** - Generates search queries for relevant stock footage
4. **API Integration** - Fetches clips from Pexels, Pixabay, Unsplash (all free)
5. **Strategic Insertion** - Adds 2-4 second clips at natural break points
6. **User Preview** - Shows exactly what was added with option to remove
7. **Final Render** - Assembles video with approved enhancements

### Rules & Limits
- **Maximum:** 15% of total video duration
- **Purpose:** Transitions, B-roll, establishing shots only
- **Priority:** User's media ALWAYS takes precedence
- **Transparency:** All stock footage clearly labeled
- **Control:** User can remove any or all stock clips
- **Quality:** Only contextually relevant, professional footage
